Pasta Amore

About

Pasta Amore is a traditional family restaurant with an original passed down recipe through generations. The recipe was created by a small italian family back in the 1900s in brooklyn. The parents dream was to want to spread their special recipe to others who would love pasta and had eventually been made into the franchise known today as Pasta Amore. Society wasn't as accepted of their food at first not wanting to try it thinking it would be any kind other kind of pasta but then word had started getting around about the pasta being different and original. Now today there are restaurants everywhere in the world and almost every single town has one. We hope to continue to spread our love in pasta form around society to pasta lovers everywhere!
